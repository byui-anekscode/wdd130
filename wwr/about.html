Ultimate Nigeria Rafting

Get ready to paddle through the heart of Nigeria's breathtaking rivers with Ultimate Nigeria Rafting, your gateway to an unforgettable journey. Whether you're gliding along the serene waters of the Niger River or conquering the rapids of the Benue, we've got the perfect adventure for YOU!

History

Founded in 2020, Ultimate Nigeria Rafting was created to bring the thrill of whitewater adventures to Nigeria's iconic rivers, showcasing the nation's natural beauty and diverse landscapes. With a mission to promote eco-tourism and outdoor fun, the company quickly became a top choice for both locals and tourists seeking unforgettable rafting experiences.
