Nigeria

Nigeria, often called the "Giant of Africa," is a vibrant and culturally rich country located in West Africa. It is Africa's most populous nation, with over 200 million people, and boasts a diverse tapestry of ethnic groups, including the Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, and over 250 others. Known for its bustling cities like Lagos and Abuja, Nigeria is an economic powerhouse, driven by oil exports, agriculture, and a rapidly growing tech industry. The country is celebrated for its dynamic arts, Nollywood film industry, Afrobeat music, and natural wonders such as the Niger River and Yankari National Park. Despite challenges, Nigerians are renowned for their resilience, innovation, and unwavering optimism.
